St. Augustine

Established in 1565, St. Augustine is the USA's first city! It was first settled by the Spaniards which then lead to a back and forth battle between the English before eventually becoming part of a state of the United States. Here, you will find a historic city that is full of excellent sea food, scenic beaches, and mostly clear, sunny skies.
